1. Privacy Policy

Introduction
Red-F Studios ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains how FlashFeed handles your data.

Data Collection & Storage

  • No Personal Data: We do not collect names, emails, phone numbers, location, or device IDs.
  • Local Storage Only: All data (bookmarks, settings) is stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences and Hive. If you uninstall the app, this data is lost.
  • No External Servers: We do not operate a backend server and do not transmit your data to the cloud.
  • No Third-Party Tracking: There are no analytics, crash reporting, or advertising SDKs in this app.

Permissions

We only request Internet permission (android.permission.INTERNET) to fetch public RSS feeds. We do not access your camera, contacts, or file storage.

Age Restriction (18+)

FlashFeed is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from individuals under 18.
