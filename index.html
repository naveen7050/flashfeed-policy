1. Privacy Policy

Introduction
FlashFeed is an RSS-based news reader developed and published by Red-F Studios. This Privacy Policy explains what data the app accesses, how data is handled, and your rights. By using FlashFeed you agree to this policy.

Information We Collect

  • No Personal Data: FlashFeed does not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, location, device identifiers, or other personal identifiers.
  • No Accounts: The app does not provide account creation, authentication, or sign-in features.
  • No Analytics / Crash Reporting: FlashFeed does not include analytics SDKs or crash-reporting tools (e.g., Firebase Analytics, Crashlytics, Sentry).
  • Local Storage Only: The app stores onboarding flags using SharedPreferences and user bookmarks using Hive. This data is stored locally on the user’s device and is not transmitted to any server.

How the App Works

FlashFeed fetches publicly available RSS/Atom XML feeds directly from publisher servers over the network. The app displays headlines, summaries, and links; clicking an item opens or links to the original publisher. FlashFeed does not modify or re-publish third-party content beyond displaying it as provided by the feed.

Data Sharing

FlashFeed does not share, sell, or rent any user data. No information is transmitted to Red-F Studios servers or to third parties. All user data stored by the app remains on the device unless the user manually exports it.

Permissions

The app requests only the following Android permission:

  • android.permission.INTERNET — required to fetch RSS feeds.

No storage, camera, location, contacts, or notification permissions are used by the app in its current form.

Target Audience

FlashFeed is primarily intended for users 18 years and older. However, the app is open to general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Retention & Security

Local data (bookmarks, settings) is retained on the device. Uninstalling the app will remove that data. Because no personal data is sent to servers, there is no remote data retention by Red-F Studios.

Contact

If you have questions or concerns about privacy, contact Red-F Studios at: redfstudio.apps@gmail.com.
